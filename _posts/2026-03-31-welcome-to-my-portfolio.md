Hello World! welcome to my portfolio site.

This is my personal space where I will share my work, thoughts, and insights on cyber security and software development.

My focus is on understanding how systems behave in real-world conditions—how they fail, how they can be exploited, and how they can be built more securely. The content here is driven by hands-on practice, experimentation, and continuous learning.

## What You'll Find Here

- **Security Research** — Practical insights into vulnerabilities, misconfigurations, and attack surfaces, along with approaches to identifying and mitigating them.
- **Software Engineering** — Thoughtful approaches to building reliable, maintainable, and secure systems, with an emphasis on clarity and structure.
- **Tools & Workflows** — Exploration of tools, techniques, and processes that improve efficiency in both development and security testing.
- **Learning in Public** — Notes, breakdowns, and reflections from ongoing practice, including challenges, experiments, and lessons learned.

## Why I Built This

This portfolio serves as a record of my progress and a place to share practical knowledge. I created this space to document my learning journey, contribute to the tech community, and connect with others who are passionate about security and engineering.

Whether you're a fellow security researcher, a developer interested in writing more secure code, or simply curious about technology, I hope you'll find something useful here.

## What's Coming

Expect regular posts on:

- Technical write-ups based on hands-on work.
- Clear breakdowns of problems and solutions.
- Practical insights into security and system design.
- Ongoing updates as I continue to learn and build.

Thanks for visiting. Feel free to explore the site, and don't hesitate to reach out if you'd like to collaborate or discuss technology.
